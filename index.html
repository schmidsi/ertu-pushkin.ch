«Ertu & Pushkin» is a producer and DJ duo from Zurich, Switzerland. the two members are also known as «Ertu Denli and Pushking Noize». But «Ertu & Pushkin» is shorter, better and sexier. They play and produce electronic music, some of the tunes are really hard electro, some have tropical influences and some are just nice to dance to.
