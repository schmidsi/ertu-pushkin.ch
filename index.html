In his youth, Pushkin once made a mix and sent it to a music blog. That’s how he got his first booking although he couldn’t dj at all. Smart as he was, he asked an experienced DJ to help him out: Ertu. This fella liked the deal cause he got offered half the booking fee and a bottle of the finest Vodka. Ertu and Pushkin was born.

5 Years later, they created the official birthday-track for the label they grew big with: Nasty Trash. Ever since, Ertu & Pushkin have accompanied this label as resident DJs and grew together. During this time, they got the chance to play along with Major Lazer, Diplo, Dada Life, Dillon Francis, Crookers, Flosstradamus, Zeds Dead and many more. But beside being drunk and spinning tracks in clubs and at festivals, they spent ages in front of the computer. So besides reaching the highscores on Mario Kart, they created tracks you can hear now on labels like Shax Trax, Crux Records, Tropicalbass.com, Oh Wow Records and Riotriot Records. Right now the two are creating their debut EP.

Over the years they developed their own music genre. It’s mainly EDM big room spiced up with Global Bass and Trap, but you would always be prepared fort hem to drop a good ol' Hip-Hop or Dancehall bomb to push the crowd to their limits. At the end of the day, they’re just two lads who just love positive vibes and with a deep honest desire to make a party a real fucking party with wavin’ flags, throwing cats and stuff. #waveyaflags #yolo #swag.
